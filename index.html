Meet Our Directors

Maria Thereza Ledochwska

Maria Thereza Ledochwska is the visionary CEO of our college. Her dedication to education, leadership, and community service inspires both staff and students. Under her guidance, Maria Thereza Ledochwska College continues to excel in academic and social development.

Why Maria Thereza Ledochwska College?

  • Personalized Attention: We offer individual attention which helps the learner to focus and sustain attention, thereby making the learning process more effective and enjoyable.
  • Supportive Environment: Our college fosters a caring and supportive atmosphere where every student feels valued and motivated to achieve their best.
  • Holistic Development: We believe in nurturing not just academic excellence but also social, emotional, and ethical growth.
  • Experienced Educators: Our dedicated staff are passionate about teaching and committed to the success of every student.
  • Modern Facilities: We provide up-to-date resources and facilities to enhance the learning experience.

Student Clubs

Drama Club: Express creativity through acting, script writing, and stage performance.

Sports Club: Participate in football, basketball, athletics, and more for fitness and teamwork.

Robotics Club: Build and program robots, compete in tech challenges, and learn coding.

Art Club: Explore painting, drawing, sculpture, and creative design projects.

Music Club: Join the choir, band, or learn instruments and music production.

Charity Club: Engage in community service, fundraising, and helping those in need.

Leadership Club: Develop leadership skills, organize events, and represent the student body.

Environment Club: Promote sustainability, tree planting, and environmental awareness.

Cooking Club: Learn culinary skills, nutrition, and participate in cooking competitions.

Debate Club: Sharpen public speaking, critical thinking, and argumentation skills.

Outside Tours & Excursions

Educational Tours: Visits to museums, science centers, and historical sites to enhance classroom learning.

Nature Trips: Explore national parks, botanical gardens, and wildlife sanctuaries for environmental education.

City Tours: Discover local culture, government institutions, and urban development projects.

Camping: Overnight camps for team building, adventure, and outdoor skills development.

Community Outreach: Participate in service projects and outreach programs to give back to society.
